Who is Joe King? Know His Unopned Personal Life Story

Joe King’s real name is Joseph Aaron King was born on 25 May 1980 in Colorado. He is an American singer, guitarist, songwriter and co-founder of Denver-based rock band The Fray. He gets an education from Faith Christin Academy in Arvada. He also worked as an auto damage appraiser before he join Fray.

At a very short age, he starts playing piano, but after some time he starts playing guitar. And he lurches guitar because he said “The coolest guys in my eighth-grade class all played guitar. I wanted to fit in.” Fray’s first release was Movement EP in which Joe sings two songs  “It’s for You” and “Where You Want To.” He dedicated his one song to his sister, “Heaven Forbid.”

King said about his band:

“With success, you have a lot more drama. For me, it was extreme highs in my career and extreme lows in a relationship. There were really obvious things to write about.”

At the time of the band’s second album King said:

 “A lot of really big realizations about ourselves are on this record. We’re singing about real things that we’ve experienced. I’m not really comfortable talking about it, but I’m a lot more comfortable singing about it. It’s a different side of me.”

When he was only 19 he got married to Julie and the couple had two daughters Elise Madison King and Ava Julie King. After their divorce, he starts dating Candice Accola, whom he met during a Super Bowl event in 2012. After two years in 2014, they married and the couple have also two children. In 2022 both were split off.
