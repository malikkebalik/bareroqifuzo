Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers reveal that Gabi Hernandez DiMera (last played by Camila Banus) was thrown in prison to await trial for a crime she didn’t commit. However, there could be hope for Gabi’s future based on some new social media leaks.

Instagram has become a great source for DOOL casting hints.

When an actor is following several Days cast members, it can often be a clue that they’ve joined the show and will be making a debut down the road.

That brings us to Cherie Jimenez, who went by the name Cherie Daly in some of her previous roles.

Jimenez’s Instagram account indicates that she’s following multiple people connected to Days, including Brandon Barash (Stefan DiMera), Carson Boatman (Johnny DiMera) and Raven Bowens (Chanel Dupree).

Jimenez is also following the Peacock account and even Camila Banus herself, so all signs point to her making her DOOL debut in the coming months.

Maybe Jimenez has been relying on Banus for tips to help with the Gabi recast transition.

It’s possible that Days could bring Jimenez on as another character instead, but it makes the most sense for her to be the new Gabi Hernandez DiMera.

Of course, it’s also possible someone else scored the role in the end, but these Instagram follows are certainly something to pay attention to.
