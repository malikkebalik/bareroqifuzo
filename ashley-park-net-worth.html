Ashley Park Net Worth

Ashley Park:

$500 thousand

Ashley Park is an American actress who is both active on stage and on screen. She debuted as an actress on the stage production of Miss Saigon in 2009. In 2012, she made her film debut with a small role in Charlene Kaye: Human. Her first credited role was in the film The V Card. In 2014, she had her first television appearance in an episode of My Dirty Little Secret. More recently, she became known for her recurring role as Olivia Cho on the sitcom Nightcap. This year, she appeared in an episode of Olivia Cho. She will also appear in the upcoming Netflix miniseries Tales of the City.
