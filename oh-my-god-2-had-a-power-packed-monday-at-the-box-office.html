Oh My God 2 had a power-packed Monday at the box office

Oh My God 2 had a power-packed Monday at the box office

Published on Aug 16, 2023 12:46 am IST


The comedy-drama Oh My God 2 has indeed surprised many with its opening-day collections. Though the hype was minimal, the film is going from strength to strength with each passing day due to its powerful and relevant content which is regarding sex education.

After having collected 43 crores in its first weekend, the film maintained a strong grip on day four and collected an impressive 12 crores nett. The first Monday collections are higher than the opening day, which tells us that the audiences are lapping up the film.

This Akshay Kumar starrer has crossed the 50 crores mark and will soon join the 100 crores club. Akshay Kumar’s charming screen presence took the film to the next level. Pankaj Tripathi and Yami Gautam’s superb performances and Amit Rai’s direction are being lauded by one and all.
