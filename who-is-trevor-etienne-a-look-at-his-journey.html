Who is Trevor Etienne? A Look at His Journey!

On July 9, 2004, Trevor Etienne was born and enrolled at Louisiana’s Jennings High School. Etienne rushed for 2,455 yards and 34 touchdowns in his high school career. He amassed 1,344 running yards and 20 touchdowns as a senior, earning a spot on the All-American Bowl roster.

Etienne, a standout running back in the 2022 class, chose the University of Florida over offers from Clemson and LSU to play collegiate football.

Why is Trevor Etienne Famous?

Trevor Etienne plays American football for the Florida Gators, a running back. Etienne gained 63 yards on five runs in his debut collegiate game before scoring his first rushing touchdown the following week against Kentucky. He would rush for the winning score in a 31–28 victory over USF.

Etienne will later score touchdowns against Georgia and LSU. He was selected to the SEC All-Freshman Team in 2022 after amassing 719 yards and six touchdowns on the ground as a freshman.

Trevor Etienne Signs NIL Deal with Gataverse

Etienne had the fourth most yards rushing by a true rookie tailback in the history of the school. Now in his second year, he and Montrell Johnson Jr. are expected to be one of the best running back teams in the country.

Etienne has signed a NIL deal with the OpenLocker fan group Gataverse before the first game of the season on Thursday, September 1, 2023 night at Utah. The company has its own NFT platform and uses blockchain technology to make authentic digital collectables for sports.

Gataverse gave the sophomore his own limited edition collectable, which also serves as proof that he is a part of the community. The signed card costs $77 and lets you meet Travis Etienne’s younger brother. Fans who buy the card will have a chance to meet Etienne and will also get a 20% discount at Bleu Bird Wings.

For every Florida win in which he scores two touchdowns, cardholders can get seven wings, fries and a drink at a Gainesville restaurant. The seven is a reference to the number on the running back’s jersey.

Gataverse community manager Matt Ulino said in a statement:

“The Trevor Etienne Collection represents the next level of NIL which is designed to drive fan engagement with real-world utility tied to athlete performance, We’ve already seen how excited Gator fans are to get something tangible and meet their favorite athlete at an event. Local merchants like Bleu Bird will bring the Gataverse community together by creating opportunities for fans to earn rewards and experiences.”
