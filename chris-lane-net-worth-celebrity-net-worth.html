What is Chris Lane's Net Worth?

Chris Lane is an American country music singer and songwriter who has a net worth of $2 million. Chris Lane was born in Kernersville, North Carolina in November 1984. His debut studio album Let's Ride (with Chris Lane Band) was released in 2012 and reached #75 on the US Country Chart. Lane released the album Girl Problems in 2016 which reached #8 on the US Country chart and #55 on the Billboard 200 chart.

His album Laps Around the Sun was released in 2018 and also reached #8 on the US Country chart as well as #83 on the Billboard 200 chart and #51 in Canada. Chris Lane also released the EP Fix in 2015 which reached #4 on the US Heatseekers chart. His single "Fix" reached #1 on the US Country Airplay chart, #10 on the US Country chart, and #8 on the Canadian Country chart. His single "For Her" reached #1 on the Canadian Country chart and he also had success with the single "Take Back Home Girl" (featuring Tori Kelly).
