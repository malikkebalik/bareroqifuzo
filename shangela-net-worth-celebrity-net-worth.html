What is Shangela's Net Worth?

Shangela is an American drag queen and reality television personality who has a net worth of $1 million. Shangela was born in Paris, Texas. He is best known for competing on the reality television series RuPaul's Drag Race and was the first contestant to be eliminated in season two and then finished sixth in season three. Shangela also competed on the third season of RuPaul's Drag Race: All Stars.

As an actress he appeared in the films R.I.P.D, Kiss Me, Kill Me, Hurricane Bianca, Hurricane Bianca 2: From Russia with Hate, and A Star Is Born. Shangela has also appeared in episodes of the TV series Detroit 1-8-7, Dance Moms, Community, Toddlers & Tiaras, The Soup, The Mentalist, 2 Broke Girls, Glee, The Bold and the Beautiful, Bones, The X-Files, and more. He was featured on the single "Kitty Girl" by RuPaul featuring BeBe Zahara Benet, Kennedy Davenport, and Trixie Mattel which reached #18 on the US Dance chart.
