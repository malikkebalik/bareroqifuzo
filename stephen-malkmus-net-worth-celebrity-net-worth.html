What is Stephen Malkmus' Net Worth?

Stephen Malkmus is an American musician and singer who has a net worth of $5 million. Stephen Malkmus was born in Santa Monica, California in May 1966. He is best known for being the lead singer and guitarist of the indie rock band Pavement as well as Stephen Malkmus and the Jicks. Pavement formed in 1989 and released their debut album Slanted and Enchanted in 1992. The band released the albums Crooked Rain, Crooked Rai in 1994, Wowee Zowee in 1995, Brighten the Corners in 1997, and Terror Twilight in 1999.

Their single "Cut Your Hair" reached #10 on the US Modern Rock chart. Pavement broke up in 1999 but reunited in 2010. Stephen Malkmus and the Jicks formed in 2000 and released the albums Stephen Malkmus in 2001, Pig Lib in 2003, Face the Truth in 2005, Real Emotional Trash in 2008, Mirror Traffic in 2001, and Wig Out at Jagbags in 2014. Malkmus has also released albums with Silver Jews and The Crust Brothers.
