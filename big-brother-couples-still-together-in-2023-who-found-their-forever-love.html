Big Brother Couples Still Together in 2023: Who Found Their Forever Love?

Big Brother U.S. wrapped up airing its 24th season in September 2022, with Taylor Hale claiming the grand prize and creating reality show history. Like previous seasons, this one featured intense competition, emotional highs and lows, and plenty of drama.

It’s common knowledge that couples who met on reality TV rarely stay together for very long. Constant close quarters bring out the rawest and most intense emotions in the contestants. Most of the couples who developed during the show are no longer together because romance tends to burn out once people return to the real world. Love, though, can sometimes last forever.

Only ten of the many relationships that began in the Big Brother house (or “showmances,” as the public dubs them) are still going strong as of this writing.
