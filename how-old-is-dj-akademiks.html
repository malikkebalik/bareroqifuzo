How old is DJ Akademiks?

DJ Akademiks was born on 17 May 1991, in Spanish Town, Jamaica. While enrolling at Rutgers University, he simultaneously worked as a disc jockey while pursuing a degree in bio-mathematics. He also started a website called Late Night Creep that focused on hip-hop music news. He has a bachelor’s degree in biomathematics and was born and raised in Jamaica before moving to the US.

DJ Akademiks launched his own website, Late Night Creep, while he was still a student at Rutgers. He covered hip-hop music news on it. Later, he branched out into the urban entertainment sector, uploading humorous and satirical movies on YouTube. In 2014, his YouTube channel began to grow significantly in prominence, in part because of his coverage of the Chicago drill music scene. Presently he has five Youtube channels with millions of subscribers.
