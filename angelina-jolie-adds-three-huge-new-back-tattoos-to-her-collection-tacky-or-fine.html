Angelina Jolie adds three huge new back tattoos to her collection: tacky or fine?

I’ve always been sort of meh on Angelina Jolie’s tattoos. You would have thought that after the years of laser treatments to remove the “Billy Bob” tattoo, Angelina would be tattoo-shy. But no, she just keeps adding to her collection. Jolie is still in Cambodia, directing a movie. And she was photographed over the weekend in some kind of tank top, thus we get to see that Jolie has added three more massive tattoos to the collection on her back.

Many people are mystified as to the significance of these new tattoos. I swear to God, one of them (about halfway down her back) looks like part of the periodic table. Let’s see… the tattoo on her left shoulder is the one she’s had for years, and it’s a Cambodian prayer. Some of the new tattoos are apparently yantra (sak yant) style, a traditional Buddhist tattoo style. People Magazine’s source says Jolie did get “three new tattoos and that they are Buddhist blessings for peace, love and a prosperous life.”

My favorite of all of Angelina’s tattoos? I have two favorites – I love the latitude/longitude lines of her children’s birth places, and I love her “Whiskey Bravo” tattoo on her thigh for Brad (William Bradley = Whiskey Bravo).
