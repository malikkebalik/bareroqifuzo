Katt Williams Said His Height Made Him Try Harder With Women, But 'Short Guys Make Better Lovers'

Celebrity

Katt Williams might have tried harder with women because of his height, but the comedian believes being shorter makes men 'better lovers'.

Published on January 30, 2022

3 min read

Katt Williams started his career as a stand-up comedian and has done several comedy specials. He picked out his stage name after an injury that led to him wearing hats on stage. Williams later would get into acting, and his appearances on screen helped him gain recognition.

The 50-year-old star has been through several relationships over the years. Williams talked about his attempts to be successful with women. He put in more effort due to not being tall enough to meet some women’s standards.

Katt Williams tried harder due to his height

It would seem that Williams’ height affected how he navigated his previous relationships. He once spoke with Conan O’Brien on Late Night with Conan O’Brien. The two of them started discussing Williams’ height, and the actor joked about waiting for his growth spurt. 

Williams appeared good-natured about not being as tall as other men. O’Brien steered the conversation to ask him if his stature impacted his romantic relationships. Williams explained that he tried hard to be successful with women. The reason was that he could not fulfill the requirement of “tall, dark, and handsome.” 

“When I got a woman, I really tried hard. You know?” Williams told O’Brien. “I think short guys make better lovers. Just ’cause once we get on, we don’t want to get off.”

Williams also discussed what it was like dating tall women like the WNBA player. He expressed that it was a weird experience. On the one hand, he was pleased that height didn’t matter to them. Still, he felt like he was a child holding his mother’s hand.

Who has Katt Williams dated?

Williams has had multiple relationships in the past. According to Affair Post, the comedian used to be married to a woman named Quadirah Locus. The couple had a kid together, but they ended up getting a divorce in 1995. Williams later went on to marry Eboni Gray for a short while.

For a couple of years, Williams was in a relationship with a rapper named Arica Kane. Some people might know her as Hazel E, and she is one of Williams’ most high-profile romances. The two were together from 2014 to 2016.

Later in 2016, Williams had a romance with R&B singer Jhonni Blaze. Blaze was a part of Growing Up Hip Hop and the Love & Hip Hop franchise. She once appeared in Tory Lanez’s Instagram Live. Of course, there have been rumors of other past girlfriends and various flings.

It is unknown what the heights of Williams’ previous partners are. However, he once mentioned that he dated a WNBA player who was 6’3″. However, he did not say the name of the woman. 

How many kids does Katt Williams have?

There is not much information about who Williams is currently dating. It would seem that he possibly is single at the moment. However, the comedian does have a family consisting of many children. Williams only has one biological son named Micah Williams.

Williams gained custody of Micah when he got a divorce from his first wife, Locus. Most reports mentioned that he adopted seven children over the years. There is no mention as to the names of the individual kids. In total, he is the father of eight children.

However, Williams once mentioned that he adopted six kids. Despite the varying reports, he wanted to take in more children as a way of helping people.
