Snoop Dogg Munchie Meal Unleashes Late-Night Delights at Jack in the Box

Snoop Dogg and Jack in the Box have teamed together to create a new meal called “Snoop’s Munchie Meal,” and both are huge in California.

Jack in the Box announced in a press release that from now through August 6, customers can get a combo meal for $14 that includes a sampling of their most popular menu items and sauces. Jack in the Box and Snoop Dogg both understand late-night appetites, according to the statement.

Can You Really Get a Snoop Meal at Any Jack in the Box Restaurant in the Country?

Jack in the Box, based in San Diego, said that they will be releasing commercials featuring legendary hip-hop musician Snoop Dogg throughout the month to promote the new menu item.

Snoop’s Munchie Meal “has it all,” the statement read.

“Whether you’re feeling baked, sauced, loaded (or all of the above), Snoop’s Munchie Meal has it all.”

From June 12 through August 6, you may get Snoop’s Munchie Meal at any Jack in the Box location, online via Jack’s website, or with the Jack app. More than two thousand Jack in the Box restaurants may be found in America.
