Power

  • The non-DPF turbo-charged engine delivers constant high levels of torque, regardless of the elevation.
  • Four different power models can be selected to let E145 operators be able to adapt to various projects.
  • The power boost button temporarily increases hydraulic power when users require breaking through the solid ground.
  • The machine can be outfitted with an extra dozer blade to provide excellent stability in digging and levelling capabilities.

Durability and Security

  • Designing the frame with D channels on E145’s upper structure provides strength to resist side impacts better and safeguard the machine’s components.
  • Wear plates that are hard and wear-resistant on the pins connecting to the attachment to prevent side play during long time intervals of service on bucket pin-up points
  • Track pins are sealed for life and never require lubrication.
  • A belt tensioner automatic keeps the tension of the accessory belt for the engine.
  • Pin bosses made of reinforced cast steel give additional strength to the demanding work conditions.
  • Features several options to ensure the machine’s security against vandalism or theft. These include security by passcode, master battery disconnect with lockable panels, and lockable fuel filling points.

Comfort and Easy Component Access

  • The pressurized cab is equipped with an oblique sound-isolating seal that helps ensure that sound levels are optimal within the operator stations.
  • The large cabin provides ample access for operators to the sides and front of the machine.
  • Other comfort features include an air-suspension seat, a top air conditioning and heating system, a simple-to-read 7-inch LCD screen, and regular rearview and side-view cameras.
  • Access panels are wide and allow access to the top of the E145 engine. The belly pan is hinged to permit to access the machine from below.
  • Steel side panels give access to routine maintenance items that make upkeep easy and greater uptime security.
  • Centralized grease banks are easily accessible from the ground for essential daily maintenance easy and fast.
