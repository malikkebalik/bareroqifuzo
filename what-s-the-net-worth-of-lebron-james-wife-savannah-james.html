With time flying by so quick, it may be easy to forget that NBA star LeBron James and his wife, Savannah James, have been married for so long. The two have been the talk of the interwebs after showing out at their son’s recent basketball game.

How long have LeBron and Savannah James been together and when did they meet?

LeBron and Savannah James have been together for nearly two decades. They both grew up in Akron, Ohio and met when they were teenagers. They attended rival high schools. According to the pair, their first date was at Outback Steakhouse. It was easy for them to stay together once he was drafted by the Cleveland Cavaliers back in 2003.

They have three children together: LeBron Jr. (“Bronny”), Bryce, and Zhuri. They officially tied the knot in 2013.

What does Savannah James do for a living?

Savannah is an entrepreneur and has created multiple businesses and organizations. When she and LeBron were in Miami, Savannah also had her own juice bar called The Juice Shop. When the couple moved from Miami back to Cleveland, she closed the store.

She has also started a mentorship program for young girls called Women of Our Future, which is “a program that provides one-on-one counseling and support from screened volunteer mentors for ninth- and 10th-grade girls.” According to Celebrity Net Worth, Savannah has a net worth of $50 million.

People can’t stop talking about this photo of LeBron and Savannah James

A photo of LeBron and Savannah at Bronny’s basketball game is the talk of the internet. The parents look super excited and supportive of their son like any over-the-top parents would be at their child’s sporting event. The game was even more special than usual because Bronny’s school was playing LeBron’s alma mater.

“This video of Lebron and Savannah supporting bronny is too adorable #goals,” tweeted one person. “These shots of Lebron and Savannah watching their son, Bronny, play against his dad’s old high school are EVERYTHING. This can most definitely be a goal,” said another.

Another person added, “I remember when LeBron’s mom would get so hyped to watch LeBron play. Wow…now it’s LeBron and Savannah’s turn. They look so young! Savannah’s expression tickles me!”

This energy from LeBron and Savannah is definitely something to strive for as we head into 2020.
