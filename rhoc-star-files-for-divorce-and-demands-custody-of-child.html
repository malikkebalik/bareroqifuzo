'RHOC' Star Files For Divorce and Demands Custody of Child

In case you missed it, there was a recent cast shakeup to The Real Housewives of Orange County. Kelly Dodd, Braunwyn Windham-Burke, and Emily Simpson were let go from the cast, and Bravo brought on Noella Bergener, Nicole James, and Dr. Jennifer Armstrong. The network also decided to have Heather Dubrow reprise her role as a housewife.

We’re sure to get plenty of new drama with the new cast, but it looks like it’s already getting started!

Noella Bergener has filed for divorce from her husband, James Bergener. She is also demanding legal and physical custody of their 2-year-old son.
