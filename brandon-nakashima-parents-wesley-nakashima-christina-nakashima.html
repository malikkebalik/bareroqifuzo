Brandon Nakashima parents: Wesley Nakashima, Christina Nakashima

Brandon Nakashima, born August 3, 2001, is a professional tennis player from the United States. The Association of Tennis Professionals has him rated as high as world No. 62 in singles, which he first attained in November 2021.

In the same month, he hit a career-high of No. 396 in doubles.

Nakashima was ranked as high as No. 3 in the world as a junior.

He won two championships on the ITF junior circuit in 2018 and went on to win the ITF Junior Masters, the year-end competition for the top junior singles players.

He eventually enrolled at the University of Virginia to play collegiate tennis, but after his first year, he decided to forego his remaining years of eligibility.

Nakashima made his professional debut in August 2021, after reaching his first two ATP Tour finals.
