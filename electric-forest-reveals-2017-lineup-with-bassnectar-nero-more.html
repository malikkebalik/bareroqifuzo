Electric Forest Reveals 2017 Lineup With Bassnectar, Nero &amp; More

Recommended Videos

Electric Forest has announced the first wave of their 2017 lineup, revealing a stacked talent roster for the wooded festival’s newly expanded format for next year. Back in September, the festival organizers behind Electric Forest announced that 2017 would see the event extended across a second weekend, and the results definitely do not disappoint.

Across the two weekends, Electric Forest has organized an enticing mix of performers for the upcoming festival, with a number of acts slated to play on both lineups. The String Cheese Incident and Bassnectar will headline at both weekends, while DJ Snake, ODESZA and Above & Beyond will make appearances as well.

In terms of the supporting cast, we have a number of great artists like Nero, A-Trak, Illenium, REZZ, 12th Planet, Big Gigantic, Alison Wonderland and more comprising the bill. The lineup is put together in such a way as to make it worth it for fans to spring for both weekends, with plenty of opportunity to catch favorites for both dates while keeping it interesting with enough variation.

Electric Forest 2017 will kick off in Rothbury, Michigan on June 22-25, and then resume the following weekend for June 29-July 2. You can check out the roster for both weekends below.
