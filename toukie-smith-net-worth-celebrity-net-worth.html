What is Toukie Smith's Net Worth?

Toukie Smith is an American actress and model who has a net worth of $3 million. Toukie Smith was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in April 1955. She is best known for starring as Eva Rawley on the NBC television series 227 from 1989 to 1990. Her brother was the fashion designer Willi Smith. As a model Toukie worked for Versace, Chanel, and many other brands.

She appeared for Yves St. Laurent and in the magazines Vogue, Ebony, ELLE, Cosmopolitan, Redbook, and Seventeen. She had a recurring role as Angie in the TV series Miami Vice from 1987 to 1988. Smith also starred in the movies Me and Him, Talkin' Dirty After Dark, I Like It Like That, Joe's Apartment, The Preacher's Wife, and Goosed. Toukie dated Robert De Niro from 1988 to 1996.
