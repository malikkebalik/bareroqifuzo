Nikki Amuka Bird, Who Played Sabrina in “Knock at the Cabin”

Nikki Amuka Bird (born on February 27, 1976, age 47 years) is a Nigerian-born actress who is currently based in London, UK. You might have seen her playing Rav Mulcair in the sci-fi comedy “Avenue 5” alongside artists like Hugh Laurie, Josh Gad, Zach Woods, and Suzy Nakamura. Nikki has also worked on numerous blockbuster movies, including “Jupiter Ascending,” “Denial,” “The Children Act,” and “A Private War.”

These days Amuka is earning the limelight for landing the part of Sabrina in Dave Bautista starer “Knock at the Cabin,” which entered cinemas on February 3, 2023. It is a horror thriller featuring Dave Bautista, Rupert Grint, Abby Quinn, Jonathan Groff, and McKenna Kerrigan.

The story of this M. Night Shyamalan-directed project revolves around a family who, while vacationing, discovers that they will have to sacrifice one of themselves to save the planet from the apocalypse.

How Old is Nikki Amuka Bird?

On February 27, 1976, Nikki Amuka Bird (age 47) was born in Nigeria. After spending a few years in her motherland, she and her mother moved to England in search of a better life. Sources say that Nikki also spent some time in Antigua and Lagos.

While studying at Frensham Heights School, she used to attend dance classes dance with a dream of becoming a professional dancer.

Sadly one day, while practicing, she suffered a back injury and decided to quit dancing and switch to acting. She joined LAMDA (London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art) and tried to make herself better at the art. She also practiced theatre art at the Royal Shakespeare Company and did numerous stage shows.

Just after finishing her studies at LAMDA, Amuka landed a minor part in season 15 of the TV series “The Bill,” which marked the start of her career. The same year she joined the cast of Michael Chaplin’s “Grafters” and played the part of Martha in four episodes.

Nikki Amuka Bird’s Career Timeline?

The year 2000 proved to be very lucky for Nicky because this year, she appeared in multiple productions, including “Forgive and Forget,” “Safe as Houses,” and “Doctors.” In the meantime, she won the heart of her fans by playing multiple roles in Jamie Annett’s “Holby City.” It took little time for her to become the producers’ first choice because of her versatility.

After earning fame on the small screen, Bird entered the film industry with a movie titled “Almost Heaven.” It was a comedy musical drama featuring Amuka in the lead role alongside “Love Actually” actress Heike Makatsch.

Just a year later, Nick appeared in her most significant project, “The Omen,” a John Moore-directed horror film that earned $120 million worldwide.

From 2010 to 2022, she did exponential progress as an actress and appeared in various hot productions, including “Silent Witness,” “Quarry,” “Doctor Who,” “Gold Digger,” “Avenue 5,” “Jupiter Ascending,” ‘The Children Act” and “Old.” Undoubtedly, Bird is exceptionally talented because she has the skills to play any role easily.

Who is Nikki Amuka Bird Husband?

While on a project in the early 2000s, Nicky met Geoffrey Streatfeild. They had an interesting chemistry which is why they started going out. The co-stars soon became lovers, and the news of their love affair was a topic of talk back then.

After a few years of dating, Amuka and Geoffrey decided to get married; the wedding took place in 2003, and a lavish party was thrown to which all the friends were invited. Unfortunately, their marriage only lasted 7 years, and in 2010, their divorce was settled.
